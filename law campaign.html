About VIT-AP School of Law
 
Pursuing your legal career at VIT-AP School of Law (VSL), one of the India's leading academic institution. VSL offers an impressively wide ranging curriculum and determined to prepare students to global legal profession and assures graduates are practice ready for today's world and putting emphasis on practical training. VSL courses are blended with business offerings and prepares graduates for taking leadership role in the business positions with sound legal background

Discussion Room
The discussion room is equipped with comfortable seats and whiteboards and projector, marker pens, with internet connectivity. Discussion room can be used by members of the moot court judges during the sessions. These rooms have been designated a Discussion Zone: Discussions, presentations, group work conversations and brainstorming for a quick meeting and consensus of decisions.
Moot Court & Legal Aid Clinic
VSL ensures students attain practical exposure through dynamic moot court training. VSL also provides state of the art facilities and provides an opportunity to students to become the leaders and champions of the Pro-bono legal work to provide access to justice to the local and needy communities.
 
Library

VIT-AP School of Law provides air conditioned spacious and fully equipped library with requisite facilities and modern technology. The library has relevant books and journals from the world renowned publications. It possess law reports with the back volumes of 10 years. Access to online legal databases such as Lexis Nexis India, AIR (Privy Council, SC  and HC), Criminal and Civil Law Journal and Manupatra.
